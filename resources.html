Women's Helpline Numbers

Women Helpline - 1091 - Emergency helpline number for women in distress in India.

Women Helpline - 181 - Toll-free helpline number for women in need of assistance or facing violence.

Crime Statistics India

National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) - Official website of the NCRB that provides crime statistics and reports for India.

Safe City Index

Safecity - Safecity is a platform that crowdmaps sexual harassment and abuse incidents in India and works towards making cities safer for women.

Self-Defense Training Centers

Krav Maga India - Offers self-defense training programs for both men and women across India.

Defence Labs - Provides self-defense training and martial arts classes in various cities of India.

National Commission for Women (NCW)

An organization dedicated to protecting and promoting the rights of women in India.

Website: https://ncw.nic.in/

Sakhi One Stop Centre

A one-stop center for women affected by violence, providing medical, legal, and psychological support.

Phone: 181
