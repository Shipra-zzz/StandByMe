    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. What is the purpose of this service?
    This service aims to provide women with the safest route recommendations between two points, prioritizing their security and well-being.
    2. How does the safest path calculation work?
    The safest path calculation takes various factors into account, including crime statistics, well-lit areas, presence of security personnel, and user feedback. It recommends the route with the lowest perceived risk.
    3. Is the safest path always the shortest route?
    No, the safest path may not always be the shortest route. Safety considerations are prioritized over distance, ensuring that women can choose routes that minimize potential risks.
    4. Can I customize the route based on my preferences?
    Yes, you can customize the route based on your preferences. The service allows you to set additional criteria, such as well-populated areas or routes with emergency services nearby.
    5. How accurate are the safety ratings?
    The safety ratings are based on comprehensive data analysis and user feedback. However, it's important to note that safety ratings are subjective and can vary based on individual experiences.
