Safety Tips

  1. Always be aware of your surroundings, especially in unfamiliar or secluded areas.
  2. Trust your instincts - if something feels off or uncomfortable, remove yourself from the situation.
  3. Keep your personal belongings secure and within sight to prevent theft.
  4. Share your whereabouts with a trusted friend or family member when going out alone.
  5. Avoid walking alone at night, and if you must, stick to well-lit and populated areas.
  6. Learn basic self-defense techniques to protect yourself if needed.
  7. Use trusted transportation services or arrange for a ride-share when traveling alone.
  8. Be cautious when sharing personal information online and adjust privacy settings on social media platforms.
  9. Keep emergency contact numbers saved on your phone and readily accessible.
  10. Attend self-defense or safety workshops to enhance your knowledge and skills.
